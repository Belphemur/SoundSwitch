# Notifications

The **Notifications** tab lets you choose how SoundSwitch alerts you when an audio device is switched or your microphone is muted.

## Notification Type

Pick the style of alert you prefer:

- **Windows Default** — Use the standard Windows toast notification.
- **Banner** — Display a custom on-screen banner.
- **Sound** — Play a sound when a device changes.
- **None** — Disable notifications entirely.

## Banner Options

If you choose the **Banner** notification type, you can customize its appearance:

- **Position** — Where the banner appears on your screen (e.g., top-right, bottom-center).
- **Opacity** — How transparent the banner is.
- **On-screen time** — How long the banner stays visible.
- **Display mode** — Show only the device name, only the icon, or both.

## Sound

- **Custom sound file** — Select your own audio file to play when a device is switched.

## Microphone Mute

- **Mute notification** — Choose whether to show a banner or play a sound when the microphone is muted or unmuted.
