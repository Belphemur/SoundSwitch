# Recording

The **Recording** tab is where you choose which audio input devices (microphones, line-in, etc.) SoundSwitch will cycle through.

## Selecting Devices

Like the Playback tab, the list shows all recording devices detected by Windows, sorted into two groups:

- **Connected** — Devices that are currently active and available.
- **Disconnected** — Devices that are currently unplugged or disabled.

Check the boxes next to the devices you want to include in the rotation. SoundSwitch will only switch between the checked devices when you use the hotkey.

## Hotkey Settings

At the bottom of the tab, you have two hotkey options:

- **Switch recording device** — Sets the hotkey for cycling through your selected recording devices (for example, `Ctrl + Alt + F7`).
- **Toggle mute** — Sets a separate hotkey to mute or unmute the default microphone instantly.
- **Enabled** — Each hotkey has its own checkbox to turn it on or off.

To set a hotkey, click its input field and press the desired key combination. SoundSwitch will register the shortcut immediately.
