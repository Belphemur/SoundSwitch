# Playback

The **Playback** tab is where you tell SoundSwitch which audio output devices (speakers, headphones, etc.) it should cycle through.

## Selecting Devices

When you open this tab, you'll see a list of all playback devices detected by Windows. The list is divided into two groups:

- **Connected** — Devices that are currently active and available.
- **Disconnected** — Devices that are currently unplugged or disabled.

To include a device in the rotation, check the box next to its name. SoundSwitch will only switch between the devices you have checked.

## Hotkey Settings

At the bottom of the tab, you can configure the global hotkey used to cycle through your selected playback devices:

- **Switch playback device** — Click the hotkey field and press the key combination you want to use (for example, `Ctrl + Alt + F11`).
- **Enabled** — Check this box to activate the hotkey. Uncheck it to disable switching.
